Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload my own file?

Not on this website. You can only link pictures that have already been uploaded onto the internet. If you would like to upload your own photo, I would suggest heading over to imgur.com and upload it there. You can then get the link to the photo by right clicking it, and copy/paste that link here.

How do I print my creation?

Save your picture by clicking the save button. Fnd your file on your computer, and print it from there.

Can I undo the color I just placed?

Although there is no direct "undo" function, you can use the right click button on your mouse to remove any color from the square you just changed.

Can I save this and come back to it later?

Unfortunately, you cannot. The save button will only save the image to your computer, not to our servers.
